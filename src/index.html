Responsive Text Size

As with Responsive Grids, text size can customized per media query breakpoint.

class="fsa-text-size--[size]@[breakpoint]", where size is one of 0 - 7, and breakpoint is one of s, m, l, or xl

Examples

I am size 1 by default, 4@m (Four at Medium), and 7@l (Seven at Large)

I am size 3 by default, 5@l (Five at Large), and 7@xl (Seven at Extra-Large)
